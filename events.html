About Special Events:


Special events at the Sammamish Farmer's Market are events that are free for anyone to attend. They are supported by special sponsors, and are designed to help improve our community.





Emerging Entrepreneurs Expo Challenge

July 10, 2024

The Emerging Entrepreneurs Expo Challenge mirrors the format of "Shark Tank" and invites youth from the Sammamish area to present their business ideas to a panel of judges from the Sammamish Chamber of Commerce. Finalists from middle school and high school, totaling five each, will vie for a cash prize generously sponsored by Key Bank, Roy Towse of Compass Realtors, and Pacific Learning Academy. Join us at the Pergola in the Commons at 6:00 pm to witness this thrilling competition unfold at the Sammamish Farmers Market.

Sponsored by Key Bank of Sammamish, Roy Towse of Compass Realty and Pacific Learning Academy.


Stay Healthy Sammamish

July 17, 2024

Discover the secrets to staying fit and healthy in Sammamish at the Sammamish Farmers Market. Chat with local health and wellness businesses who are eager to share tips and tricks for maintaining your well-being. From urgent care services, fitness experts, senior support services to wellness advice, you'll find plenty to inspire and inform you. Don't miss out on this opportunity to learn and thrive at the Sammamish Farmers Market.

Sponsored by Evergreen Health, Indigo Urgent Care, Way of Life Wellness and Aegis Living Issaquah.


Sustainable Sammamish

August 7, 2024

Sustainable Sammamish is a yearly event at the Sammamish Farmers Market. Enjoy this fun and interactive event as your family learns new and different ideas for living sustainable lives through Reduce – Re-use – Recycle, Conservation, Eating in Season, and much more.

Sponsored by Sammamish Plateau Water and Puget Sound Energy.


Non-Profit Day

August 21, 2024

Non-Profit Day invites you to explore the diverse array of organizations committed to serving our community. Delve into the ways these groups enhance our well-being and how we can offer our support in return. Whether it's aiding seniors, providing resources for teens, offering support groups for mothers, showcasing native plant gardens, or presenting volunteering opportunities, there are countless avenues to engage and create positive change.

Sponsored by Eastside Friends of Seniors.
